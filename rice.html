Rice

Description

Rice is an awesome food. You can eat it for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Yum!

Ingredients

  • As much rice as you want to eat
  • Water
  • Rice cooker

Steps

  1. Put 2 cups of rice in the rice cooker
  2. Add running water and rinse the rice with water, drain, repeat until water is clear (2-3 times)
  3. Add water to rice cooker until water reaches 1st knuckle on your finger
  4. Press the "cook" button on your rice cooker
  5. Wait for 20-30 minutes, when button switches to "warm" the rice is ready
  6. Open the rice cooker and enjoy your rice

A bowl of rice a day keeps the doctor away
