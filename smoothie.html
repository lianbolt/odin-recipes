Smoothie

Description

This is my favorite smoothie recipe. It is sooo awesome, everyone should definitely drink it!!

If you would like to learn more about the smoothie, please contact me at smoothiemaster@yum.de

Ingredients

  • 2 Bananas
  • 1/2 cup oat milk
  • 1/4 cup frozen berries (blueberries, raspberries, blackberries)
  • 1 brazil nut

Steps

  1. Put the ingredients in the blender
  2. Blend until smooth (about 45-60 seconds)
  3. Enjoy!

Hope you enjoy our smoothies!
